Understanding Element Categories

The periodic table is a systematic arrangement of chemical elements, organized by their atomic number, electron configuration, and recurring chemical properties. Below are some key categories:

  1. Metals
  2. Nonmetals
  3. Metalloids
  4. Noble Gases

Here are some examples of each category:

  • Metals: Iron (Fe), Copper (Cu), Gold (Au)
  • Nonmetals: Oxygen (O), Carbon (C), Nitrogen (N)
  • Metalloids: Silicon (Si), Boron (B), Arsenic (As)
  • Noble Gases: Helium (He), Neon (Ne), Argon (Ar)
